About this project

The Rengstorff Avenue Grade Separation (Project 22-27) will eliminate the at-grade Caltrain crossing at Rengstorff Avenue, creating either an underpass or overpass. A separate but related project, the Transit Center Grade Separation (Project 21-35), addresses the Castro Street / Evelyn Avenue crossing near the Mountain View Caltrain station. Both projects are in design phases as of early 2026.
